★ Strongest fit LINE-nativeFriendlyFree commercial
Designed by LINE specifically for their ecosystem. Why it fits MOOM: the member app is a LINE LIFF mini-app — the app already lives inside LINE on every Thai user's phone. Recommendation

Pick one of two paths:

A · Replace both with Anuphan. One family, matched metrics, dashboard-grade, Thai foundry. This is the highest-quality baseline with the fewest moving parts. Use weights 400/500/600/700. Keep the 13px body scale.

B · Split by surface: LINE Seed on member app, Anuphan on admin. The member app becomes platform-native inside LINE, the admin stays calm and data-forward. Ship both by routing font-family at the app-shell level. Slightly more ops, much stronger brand story.

Keep IBM Plex Sans Thai as a fallback after either — the glyph compatibility is a safety net.
